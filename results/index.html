Results

We are committed to upholding student privacy and safety, and hence our results data will be strictly anonymised, and no exact scores will be published, outside of the Hall of Fame. All of our data is given in quartiles. Q3 represents the top quartile, meaning that the top 25% of our students scored at or over the Q3 number. The next 25% of students scored above the Q2 number, which is also the median. Finally, the bottom 25% of students scored underneath the Q1 number. These quartile numbers are for the RAW scores only (as scaling fluctuates yearly), and have also been rounded down to the nearest integer.

Lifetime Mathematical Methods - Q1: 41, Q2: 43, Q3: 47

2025 Mathematical Methods - Q1: 39 Q2: 42 Q3: 47

Mathematical Methods scores are usually scaled up anywhere between 3 and 7 for most students, so these scores are not representative of the final scaled up score that our students achieve.

Lifetime Specialist Mathematics - Q1: 41, Q2: 42, Q3: 44

2025 Specialist Mathematics - (withheld for privacy reasons)

Specialist Mathematics scores are usually scaled up anywhere between 5 and 14 for most students, so these scores are not representative of the final scaled up score that our students achieve.

It should be acknowledged that we operate both group classes and private classes (taught by a range of different teachers), and hence score can vary between which of these classes your child decides to take. If you would like more details, you may inquire further.

We have not included metrics for lower year levels as there is no simple and standardised way to quantifiably compare our performance against the state apart from our internal metrics, which may introduce bias.
